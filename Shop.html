Linda's Journey


At this time last year I decided I'd had enough. I was over weight, incredibly unfit and struggling with painful knees, plantar fasciitis and all sorts of other aches and pains: I could no longer ignore the fact that much of it was caused by my weight.

I screwed up my courage and texted Josh to book some PT sessions - it was one of the best decisions I've made for a long time! Josh is always incredibly positive and encouraging. He has the knack of knowing exactly how far he can push me, and it's always just a little bit further than I thought possible! I really look forward to my weekly sessions - they're always a great mixture of different activities and so I never get bored and I can really see my progress over the year.

I've lost over four stones in weight, and three to four dress sizes, but perhaps the most important change is in how I feel - I'm fitter, stronger and healthier than I believed would be possible, and I know that with Josh I can continue to make progress.

If you're wondering whether Personal Training is really for you, I'd encourage you to give Josh a call and have a chat. He'll develop a programme for you and encourage you all the way. You won't regret it!
