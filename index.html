Our Story

How we met

We met in September 2011, in Manchester queuing for a nightclub in the rain. Lee and his friend were in the queue, and shouted Julia and a friend (bridesmaid Jess) to join them instead of joining the back, to help them get let in (much easier with girls!)...

A few dates later, including going to watch Calvin Harris and then Manchester United, Lee and Julia bonded over a love of music festivals and the hardship of both being the eldest of three siblings..... and the rest is history!

Soon the opportunity to work abroad beckoned, and after 6 months apart Julia joined Lee to live in the Dubai Desert.

Lee proposed 5 years later in 2016, in the rain in Bali, and of course Julia said yes!
