Company Profile

DW Consulting Engineers llp is an independent partnership formed by former principals of larger consultancies to give clients a quality of service that bigger businesses are unable to provide.

We pride ourselves on giving a personal service and enjoy good working relationships throughout our industry.

The partners and all key technical staff have worked together for many years and can offer a wealth of experience in all sectors of construction. Whilst our core skills are in the structural and civil disciplines, we also offer management services to clients where appropriate on projects with significant engineering considerations.

We operate from offices in Harrogate and Sale and aim to provide ‘local’ service to our clients across the whole of the North of England. Notwithstanding that we also operate throughout the whole of the UK on a regular basis.

Our success is dependant upon a commitment to technical excellence and a focus on identifying, meeting and delivering clients' needs.

We pride ourselves that most of our clients give us repeat business. Our reputation depends upon delivering a good service every time we are entrusted with a project; as a service provider we are only as good as our last job.
